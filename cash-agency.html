Distributed Cash Agency (CA)

Management Target

Specific kind of digital currency

LA dedicated account for CA

Qualifications and termination

Like BA, CA must pay a certain amount of digital currency deposit to LA to obtain CA qualification.

The amount of the specific deposit is determined by LA's own policy.

After qualification, the CA will obtain the CA certification information (transaction key) necessary for the LA to provide the agent with recharge.

HM will provide specialized tools for CA, based on CA's legal currency processing capabilities.

The call API interface handles the user's recharge withdrawal request automatically or semi-automatically.

Main business affairs

Set CA recharge withdrawal rate

Through the special tool provided by HM, you can bind the information of a specific digital currency exchange to automatically set.

Maintain CA Automatic Collection Interface

The competent CA can provide its own independent automatic collection interface to the LA to facilitate the automatic processing of the user's recharge request after the CA receives the payment.

Maintain CA Automatic Payment Interface

A capable CA can create and maintain an automatic payment interface on its own, so that the CA can automatically process the user's legal currency transfer information after receiving the user's withdrawal request.

Handle user recharge requests

Through the special tool provided by HM, the user's request for recharging digital currency can be processed automatically or semi-automatically.

Handle user withdrawal request

The withdrawal request of the user's digital currency conversion currency can be processed automatically or semi-automatically through a dedicated tool provided by HM.
