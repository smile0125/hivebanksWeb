Qualifications and termination

Qualifications are open.

Anyone can download the source code from Github, as long as they own the domain name, server space and database, and everyone can build their own digital asset management center.

It is also up to the administrator to decide when to terminate the service. However, after the termination of the service, any settlement of the service users or any legal liability that needs to be assumed shall not have any relationship with Main Platform.

Main business affairs

Recruit or assume digital currency agency

Main Platform recommends that LA itself or in cooperation with digital currency exchanges, individuals undertake the agency of digital currency exchange of benchmark digital assets.

Recruit or assume legal agency

Depending on the legal situation in each country, LA itself or in cooperation with banks, payment systems, and individuals undertakes the agency of converting the legal currency into a reference digital asset.

HM will provide a special BA, CA management platform for LA.

Business Model

LA's evolutionary stages of evolution

The Origin stage HM provides the Unlimited version of the LA source code to support 10,000-user users and thousands of people online.

Matrix Stage HM will provide a one-star version of the LA source code to support 100,000 users and 10,000 people online.

Queen and later HM will provide one after another.

The two-star version of the LA source code supports millions of users and 100,000 online.

The Samsung version of the LA source code supports millions of users, millions online.

Four-star and above LA versions that support more users will be assisted by HM. Samsung LA is mainly responsible for self-development.
