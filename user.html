User

Register an account

HM will prepare user multi-factor authentication information for LA. LA will select several of them as the user's registration and authentication method.

Recharge

The user selects one of the agents provided by LA to support the payment method to recharge according to his/her payment method.

The digital currency recharge is based on the current exchange rate provided by the agent.

If there is a dispute during the recharge process, relevant evidence is submitted to LA for arbitration.

Withdrawal

The user selects one of the agents provided by the LA to support the cash withdrawal method according to his withdrawal method.

According to the current exchange rate provided by the agent to withdraw cash.

If there is a dispute during withdrawal, the relevant evidence is submitted to LA for arbitration.

Transfer

The user allocates part or all of the amount in the account to other accounts that the LA manages or communicates with the LA.

LA internal transfers may charge part of the transfer fee according to the LA's respective policies.

The policy of transferring LA and HM across the LA transfer may include partial transfer processing fees.

Other digital asset hosting

The hosting method and fee collection method are determined by LA at its own discretion.

LA's Unlimited version does not support this feature temporarily.
