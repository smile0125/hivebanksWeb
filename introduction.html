What We Build

What we are going to build is an open source digital assets bank. Anyone from any part of the world can set up a digital assets bank online platform on the internet by using our open source codes. Through a developed plugin which supports a range of different languages, it can provide a convenient digital asset custody service at the digital currency unit to local user.

Just like bank customer can deposit and withdraw money, user can access digital currency and other digital assets through digital asset bank. Since digital assets can directly circulate on the Internet, digital asset bank operated by users can provide online digital asset access services even without physical retail store.

Bitcoin, the mainstream digital currency, is limited by blockchain size that induces to long transaction transfer confirmation time and high transaction fee.

Digital asset bank’s book transfer service which excludes the digital currency blockchain limitation and can accelerate the circulation of digital currency blockchainand. Due to wide varieties of digital currencies, user can freely exchange any digital currency through the digital bank's digital currency agent as well.

If local law and bank’s support are available, user can freely convert local legal currency to any digital currency via digital bank’s legal currency agent.

When global digital banks that use the same open source code are connected to each other, user can therefore easily execute the conversion of digital currency and legal currency.

It accomplish cross-border payments and transfers and result in reducing trade frictions and costs between countries and countries, regions and regions.

Difference between digital assets bank and
currency exchange

The main difference from digital currency exchange is that digital asset bank does not have the concept of placing order. User cannot set a prefer price to sell or buy a certain digital currency.

Like ordinary bank that provides currency exchange service, users can convert CNY to Dollars and verse visa.

Because it is impossible to place a fixed-price buy/sell order, people will not call foreign currency exchange bank as foreign currency exchange.

In addition, although people can convert legal currency into digital currency or convert digital currency into legal currency. unlike digital currency exchange, digital bank charge a high handling fee for customer's redemption operations to curb user's impulse for frequent tradings.

Since digital currencies are mostly issued by deflation, different from ordinary bank, digital asset bank generally adopt zero or even negative interest rate.

Therefore, digital asset bank may also charge user a digital assets custodian fee, whereas general digital currency exchange will not charge for this service.

On the other hand, digital bank does not provide futures or leveraging instrument like digital currency exchanges and it becomes more suitable for conservative investor.

Difference between digital assets bank and
digital currency wallet

In general, digital currency wallet is an application for a particular currency uses by an individual.

It is only suitable for same digital currency transfer and does not have the ability to exchange between various digital currencies nor conversion to legal currency.

And if there is a problem happened on the digital currency wallet application such as operation system failure, or individual user will face the risk of digital currency being lost or stolen if he/she uses it improperly.

In general, wallet application developer does not bear these risks; however, a powerful digital asset bank is capable to take risk in these areas.

Business model of digital assets bank

The digital asset bank can charge user a certain number of digital currency management fees as well as recharging, withdrawal, and transfer fees.

At customer’s request, digital assets can be used as collateral for legal currency loans, and legal fees and interest can collect in legal currency. Other than digital currencies, digital assets bank can also keep digital assets for their customers and charge for custodian fee.

And in the event of accident and death of customer, in accordance to the customer's personal authenticated information, digital asset bank will transfer customer's digital assets to their legal heirs.

How do digital currency and legal currency of digital assets bank work

After the digital currency agent pays a certain amount of digital currency to the digital asset bank as security deposit, and obtains digital bank’s approval, it can therefore provide services to the customer of the digital asset bank such as digital currency recharge and withdrawal operation that is within the prepaid security deposit range.

Digital currency agency agent recharge operation process:
According to the recharge rate sets by the digital currency agent at the digital assets bank, Recharging user send a certain amount of digital currency to the digital currency payment address specified by the digital currency agent.

After the digital currency agent confirms the payment is received, it will transfer -part of its balance at the digital asset bank account to the recharge user account to complete a digital currency agent recharging process.

Digital currency agency agent withdrawal operation process:
According to the withdrawal rate sets by the digital currency agent at the digital assets bank, the withdrawal user will transfer part of its balance at the digital assets bank account to the digital currency agent’s account.

After the digital currency agent confirms the payment is received, the designated number of digital currency will send to the digital currency receiving address specified by the withdrawal user, and a digital currency agent withdrawal operation is completed.

Legal currency agent operates similarly to digital currency agent. The only difference is the digital currency collection address is replaced by a legal currency collection account.

The Hive Banks project will also provide corresponding automated tools to digital currency agent and legal currency agent for facilitating automatic connection with digital asset bank.
