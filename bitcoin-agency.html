Distributed Bitcoin Agency (BA)

Management Target

Specific kind of digital currency

LA dedicated account for BA

Qualifications and termination

BA must pay a certain amount of digital currency deposit to LA to obtain BA qualification.

The amount of the specific deposit is determined by LA's own policy.

After qualification, the BA will obtain the BA certification information (transaction key) necessary for the LA to provide agent's recharging and withdrawal.

HM will provide a special tool for BA, call API interface to BA automatically handle the user's recharge withdrawal request.

Main business affairs

Set BA recharge withdrawal rate

Through the special tool provided by HM, you can bind the information of a specific digital currency exchange to automatically set.

Handling user recharge requests

Special user-supplied digital currency recharge requests can be handled automatically by HM.

Preservation of digital currency and digital assets

The user's digital currency withdrawal request can be automatically processed through a dedicated tool provided by HM.

Business Model

BA's recharge withdrawal rate

Noted

Due to the nature of the Internet's flow of digital currency across regions, a competent BA can simultaneously represent the recharge business of multiple LAs worldwide.

Therefore, BAs that represent multiple LAs at the same time are obliged to accept HM's unified management, and HM will issue relevant certifications to qualified BAs.
